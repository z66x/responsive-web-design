Tung Tung Sahur

Tung Tung Sahur is a surreal AI‑generated figure that resembles a wooden plank or anthropomorphic bat wielding a baseball bat, mimicking rhythmic “tung tung tung” percussion. The name echoes the onomatopoeia of drumbeats used in Indonesian sahur calls and sounds like a nonsensical chant from an imaginary Italian world.

This meme is often described as the strongest in VS play. The fighting video is described as having the ability to transform into a giant with wooden armor .

Tralalero Tralala

Tralalero Tralala is often credited as the “first” Italian brainrot character—a three-legged shark strutting along a beach wearing Nike sneakers, famously lip-syncing a nonsensical chant: “Tralalero tralala…” .

It is described as having waves, super jumps, fast running and strong jaw, and is expressed in rivalry with Bardiro Crocodilo.

Lirilì Larilà

Lirili Larila is visualized as a whimsical cactus‑elephant hybrid—its tall green form, rounded elephant-like head (sometimes wearing sandals), and comically expressive trunk give it a playful but bizarre charm . Some lore even grants it time-control abilities or elemental powers, pushing it into the realm of pseudo‑fantasy creatures loved by fans .

It is often described as using the clock that can stop the time in the battle. If he stopped the time, he was controversial whether he stopped himself.

Bombardiro Crocodilo

Bombardiro Crocodilo combines a crocodile’s head with the fuselage and warplane features of an old bomber aircraft, complete with nose‑art style aggression. It's often cast in pseudo‑epic military roles, piloting bomb drops or leading assault formations—frequently with absurd comedic flair.

It attacks by dropping bombs from the air and described as a strong figure. However, it seems to be weak before melee, because it shows a lot of losing to the tung tung tung tung.

Trippi Troppi

Trippi Troppi is one of the most meme-viral icons—a surreal protean creature toggling between a psychedelic shrimp‑cat hybrid and a rotund fish‑bear “King of the Sea” design . It’s said to embody “troppa trippa” (lots of tripe/fat), giving it a comedic connotation of gluttony and absurd indulgence.

They say Trippi Troppi was born from an aquarium owner accidentally dropping his entire tie-dye paint set and a tab of LSD into the shrimp tank. The Bear-Fish hybrid verison is good friends with the Cat verison.

Original Caption:
